# 🌍 IPTV Channel Collection

A curated collection of publicly available IPTV channels from around the world, organized for easy access and discovery.

## 📺 Overview

This repository brings together IPTV streams from a variety of sources, including:

* TataPlay / Airtel / SkyGo-style channel lineups
* FAST (Free Ad-Supported TV) services like Pluto TV, Sling TV, and other 24/7 streaming channels

All channels are sorted and grouped by provider, region, and content type to make navigation simple and efficient.

## 📂 Contents

* M3U playlists categorized by platform and country
* FAST channel collections (24/7 streams)
* Regional and international TV channel lists
* Frequently updated working streams
